About Us

It all began in 2010 with a small pizza store in Kilimani, Nairobi.

By 2015, 7 stores were cooking up our signature pizza experience all over Nairobi.
Today, there are more than 20 Pizza Express outlets around Kenya,
5 of which are outside Kenya. Now, Rwanda and Uganda are now a part of this international story
of pizza success.

When entertaining, a Hawaiian, a Margherita, or a Peri Peri Chicken pizza from Pizza Express is
the obvious
and perfect option to please crowds of all ages.
